                                                                                    Most people's ideas are not their own.

                                                                                    Source: Mise Mé Féin
                                                                                    Justifications:
                                                                                    • People don't put in the hard work to learn key concepts, master primary texts, make connections, or generate insights.
                                                                                    Concrete Applications:
                                                                                    • For non-current issues (i.e. whatever's not currently circulating in the media) this means that, if you can ID the sources of a personal's ideas, you're at an advantage.
                                                                                    • For current issues this means that if you focus on collecting and reading articles, reviews, etc. you can position yourself to "Gordon Wood" interlocutors, peg people's go-to sources, gain mindshare, and by strategitically injecting your own insights, influence and persuade people of all political opinions.
